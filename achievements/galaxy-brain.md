# 🦊 Galaxy Brain

> Have your answer marked as accepted in GitHub Discussions.

**Difficulty:** 🔴 Hard  
**Tiers:** 4 (Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum)  
**Time to earn:** Depends on community engagement

---

## 🏆 Tiers

| Tier | Accepted Answers |
|------|-----------------|
| 🥉 Bronze (x2) | 2 |
| 🥈 Silver (x3) | 8 |
| 🥇 Gold (x4) | 16 |
| 💎 Platinum (x5) | 32 |

---

## 📋 How to Earn It

1. Go to a repo that has **Discussions** enabled
2. Click the **Discussions** tab
3. Filter by **Q&A** category
4. Find questions with **no accepted answer** (no green ✅ checkmark)
5. Write a **clear, helpful answer**
6. Wait for the repo owner/maintainer to mark it as ✅ accepted

## ✅ Tips

- Only answer questions you **actually know the answer to**
- Write **detailed, clear answers** with examples if possible
- Look for **recent unanswered questions** — older ones may be abandoned
- The **Q&A category** is the only one that counts — not General or Ideas
- You cannot mark your **own** questions as accepted

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

- Answering in the **General** category — only **Q&A** counts
- Expecting instant results — the repo owner must manually **mark your answer**
- Answering questions that already have an accepted answer
