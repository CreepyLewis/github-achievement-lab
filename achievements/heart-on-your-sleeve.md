# ❤️ Heart On Your Sleeve

> React to something on GitHub with a ❤️ emoji.

**Difficulty:** 🟢 Easy  
**Tiers:** 1 (no tiers)  
**Time to earn:** 1 minute

---

## 📋 How to Earn It

1. Go to any **public repository** on GitHub
2. Open any **Issue**, **Pull Request**, or **Discussion**
3. Hover over any **comment**
4. Click the **😄 smiley face** icon that appears
5. Select **❤️**

Done! The reaction will appear on the comment and the achievement will follow shortly.

---

## ✅ Tips

- Works on **any comment** — issues, PRs, or discussions
- You can react on **your own comments** too
- Try a popular repo like **github.com/microsoft/vscode** — lots of comments to react to
- Achievement may take **a few hours** to appear — be patient!

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

- Using a different emoji like 👍 or 🎉 — must be specifically **❤️**
- Reacting on a **commit** directly — use issue/PR/discussion comments instead
