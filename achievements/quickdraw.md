# ⚡ Quickdraw

> Close an issue or pull request within 5 minutes of opening it.

**Difficulty:** 🟢 Easy  
**Tiers:** 1 (no tiers)  
**Time to earn:** 2 minutes

---

## 📋 How to Earn It

1. Go to any of your repositories
2. Click the **Issues** tab
3. Click **"New issue"**
4. Give it any title e.g. `Test issue`
5. Click **"Submit new issue"**
6. **Immediately** click **"Close issue"**

That's it! As long as you close it within **5 minutes** of opening, you'll earn Quickdraw!

---

## ✅ Tips

- Works with **Pull Requests** too — open one and close it within 5 minutes
- You can do it on **your own repo** — no need for other people
- Achievement may take a few minutes to appear on your profile

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

- Waiting too long before closing — must be within **5 minutes**
- Closing someone else's issue doesn't count — must be **your action**
